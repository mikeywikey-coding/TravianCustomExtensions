Travian Watchman Pro
Travian Watchman Pro is a high-performance browser extension designed to help Travian players manage their empire with precision. It automatically tracks building queues, hero movements, and incoming attacks across multiple servers, providing a real-time floating dashboard directly on your game screen.

Key Features
Smart Building Tracker
Automatic Queue Sync: Automatically detects and tracks every building in your construction queue.

Multi-Village Awareness: Labels alarms with the specific village name so you know exactly where your resources are being spent.

Hero & Combat Intelligence
Hero Lock: Simply hover over the hero overview to populate the alarm list, it cross-references coordinates in tooltips to specify if your hero is returning to a specific village or heading to an adventure/oasis.
Attack Alerts: Scans the Rally Point to highlight incoming attacks and raids with high-visibility cyan and gold indicators.
Multi-Server: Everything was designed with multi-server play in mind, it's able to manage multiple servers, multiple heros with no issues.
Advanced UI/UX
Glassmorphism Design: A sleek, semi-transparent floating widget that clears the game header.

Map Awareness: The widget intelligently hides itself when you enter Fullscreen Map mode to keep your view unobstructed.

Visual Priority System: Overdue alarms flash bright red to ensure you never miss a finished construction.

Presets
Quick Presets: Use the lightning bolt menu to instantly add 15m, 30m, or custom-timed alarms.

Recurring Alarms: Toggle the ↺ icon on any shortcut to make it automatically reset once finished—perfect for party celebrations or resource production cycles.

How to Use
1. The Construction Dashboard
As soon as you start a building, Watchman Pro detects it. The timer will appear in the floating widget on the right.
Left-Click a "DONE" timer once to silence the alarm.
Left-Click again to delete it from the list.
This is the same logic for recurring alarms, click on them twice to immediately restart them, this especially useful for farmlists. 

2. Adding Manual Alarms
Need a reminder for something specific?
Click the + button in the widget header.
Enter a Name  the duration (Hours, Minutes, Seconds).
Click enter or the checkmark to start it, these appear in a baby pink color.

3. Quick Presets
Start Timer: Click a preset name to begin a countdown immediately.
Auto-Repeat: Click the ↺ icon to toggle recurring alarms—once finished, the timer will reset automatically.
Edit: Click the pencil (✎) icon to modify an existing preset's duration or label.
Delete: Click the ✕ icon to remove a preset from your list.
Create New: Click + New to define a custom shortcut with its own label and time.

4. Clear All
Click on the red trash can icon in the header to wipe all alarms instantly.

5. Mute Toggle
Click on the speaker on the top right to quickly silence audio alerts without losing your visual timers.


Technical Details
Built With: JavaScript (ES6+), CSS Grid/Flexbox, and the WebExtensions API.
Privacy: All data is stored locally in your browser (storage.local). No data ever leaves your machine.
